About WBSA

Somali Canadian community in Alberta & Wood Buffalo.

WBSA is a community-based organization serving Somali Canadians and the wider community in Fort McMurray. We help people navigate settlement, build connections, and celebrate Somali culture across the Wood Buffalo region.

Somali Canadians in Alberta today

Somali Canadians are part of the social and economic fabric of Alberta, with families living in Edmonton, Calgary, and northern communities like Fort McMurray. Across the province, Somali-led businesses, community organizations, and cultural events help keep language, faith, and heritage strong while supporting integration.

Community strengths

  • Strong intergenerational family networks and mentorship.
  • Entrepreneurship in retail, logistics, and professional services.
  • Community leadership in education, health, and settlement support.
  • Celebrations that keep Somali language and culture vibrant.

Community priorities

  • Access to housing, childcare, and healthcare navigation.
  • Employment readiness, credential support, and training.
  • Youth leadership and academic success.
  • Maintaining cultural connection while building local belonging.

Wood Buffalo focus

Fort McMurray and the surrounding Wood Buffalo region are home to Somali families who contribute to the energy sector, small business, and public services. WBSA supports newcomers and long-time residents through practical guidance and community-building events.

Settlement navigation

Support with paperwork, appointments, referrals, and connecting to local agencies.

Family support

Programs for youth, parents, and elders that reflect Somali cultural values.

Community presence

Events and partnerships that strengthen relationships across cultures in Fort McMurray.

Our mission

Strengthen community wellbeing through support services, cultural connection, and opportunities that help people thrive.

What we do

  • Settlement navigation and referrals.
  • Youth and family programming.
  • Community events and outreach.
  • Partnerships and advocacy.
