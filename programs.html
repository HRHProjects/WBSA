Programs & services

Support for Somali Canadians in Wood Buffalo.

WBSA programs help newcomers and long-time residents navigate settlement, build community connections, and access services in Fort McMurray.

Core programs

Our services respond to current community needs in Alberta and the Wood Buffalo region.

Settlement & navigation

Guidance with paperwork, appointments, referrals, and connecting to local supports.

Youth & family programs

After-school activities, leadership programs, and family wellness supports.

Community events

Cultural celebrations and gatherings that build trust across the region.

Workshops & training

Skills sessions on employment readiness, digital literacy, and community resources.

Current focus areas

Programs are designed around the everyday goals of Somali Canadian families in Fort McMurray.

Employment navigation

Support with resumes, job search strategies, and connections to training opportunities.

Family wellbeing

Parenting support, youth mentorship, and referrals to wellness services.

Community connection

Newcomer welcome events, cultural celebrations, and interfaith partnerships.
