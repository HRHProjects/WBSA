About the Somali Canadian community

From Edmonton and Calgary to Fort McMurray, Somali Canadians are building vibrant communities rooted in mutual support, entrepreneurship, and cultural pride. WBSA highlights local stories and ensures families have access to resources in Wood Buffalo.

Read the community story

Community connection

Language, faith, and intergenerational care remain strong, with community gatherings that celebrate Somali heritage in Alberta.

Local priorities

Housing stability, youth mentorship, employment navigation, and family wellbeing are top priorities in Wood Buffalo.
