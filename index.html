About WBSA

The Wood Buffalo Somali Association is a community-based organization that provides support, connection, and programming in the Wood Buffalo region.

The Somali Canadian community contributes to the cultural and economic life of Canada while maintaining strong ties to language, heritage, and family. In Wood Buffalo, Somali residents are part of a diverse, resilient community that has grown through migration and local roots, with many families calling Fort McMurray home for work, education, and community building.

Mission

Strengthen community wellbeing through support services, cultural connection, and opportunities that help people thrive.

What we do

  • Settlement navigation and referrals
  • Youth and family programming
  • Community events and outreach
  • Partnerships and advocacy
